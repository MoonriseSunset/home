One of my long-term interests has been caring for cacti, succulents, and air plants. Here's a list of what's currently growing in my room.

## Cacti
- *Astrophytum myriostigma*  (Bishop's cap)
- *Astrophytum myriostigma*  'Onzuka'
- *Astrophytum asterias*  (Sand dollar cactus)
---
- *Stenocactus multicostatus*  (Brain cactus)
- *Stenocactus phyllacanthus* v. violaciflorus
- *Leuchtenbergia principis* (Agave cactus)

## Succulents
- *Discorea elephantipes* (Elephant's foot)
- *Dorstenia horwoodii*
- *Dorstenia crispa x foetida*
- *Gasteria* 'Little Warty'
- *Haworthia* 'Royal Albert'
- *Euphorbia obesa* (Baseball plant)
- *Lithops fulviceps*
- *Lithops* sp.

## Air plants

- *Tillandisia xerographica*

As a note and word of the wiser, air plants *really* do not like soft water, something I learned the hard way and is why the bullet above is the only species I have.

## Other plants

- *Dendrobium kingianum* (Pink rock orchid)
- *Schefflera/Heptapleurum arboricola* (Dwarf umbrella tree)
